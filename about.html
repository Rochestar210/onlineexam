About Online Examination System

This system is designed for LIMKOKWING UNIVERSITY to facilitate online examinations for various courses including:

  • Web Design
  • Business Management
  • Business Communication
  • Algebra & Mathematics
  • Programming
  • Computer Applications

The system supports three main user roles:

Administrators

Full system control, user management, and oversight of all examinations.

Examiners

Subject-specific exam creation, question management, and result evaluation.

Students

Exam participation, result viewing, and profile management.
